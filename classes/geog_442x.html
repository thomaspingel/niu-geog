GEOG 442x: Geomorphology

Keywords

Natural Environmental Systems

Prerequisites

GEOG 101 and GEOG 102, or GEOL 105, or GEOL 120, or GEOL 120 and GEOL 121.

Course Description

Crosslisted as GEOL 442. Systematic study of geologic processes affecting the evolution of the earth's surface. Emphasis on glacial, fluvial, and coastal processes and their relationship to the development of landforms under diverse climates of the past and present.

Course Format

Lecture, laboratory, and field trips.
