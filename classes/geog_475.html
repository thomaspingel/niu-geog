GEOG 475: Geomatics Planning and Computation

Keywords

Geomatics

Prerequisites

GEOG 350, GEOG 450

Course Description

Concepts and procedures for land development and land use. Discussion of common subdivision laws and municipal codes. Covenants and restrictions for protection of the public and design restrictions. Design and legal concepts of land development for miscellaneous other land development projects including industrial and recreational spaces.

Course Format

Lecture and laboratory.
